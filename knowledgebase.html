Powerful Knowledgebase Features

Multilingual Support

Got customers in multiple countries? Helpy has you covered. Multilingual support means your knowledgebase can be available and translated in all the countries where your customers are.

A modern editing interface greets you and makes it easy to write localized versions of your support articles

  • Locale Selector
  • Detects Locale from Browser
  • Help site interface is translated
  • Translation Interface in Admin

Full Text Search

The powerful search engine in Helpy helps connect your users to answers to their problems quickly and efficiently, dramatically reducing the number of customer inquiries.

New articles and documents are automatically added to the full text search engine, and submitted to Google for indexing.

  • Real time indexing
  • Search KB and Community Posts
  • Search results include snippet

Simple, clean knowledgebase editing

A modern wysiwyg editor (Trix.js) is your gateway to creating amazing articles to delight your users. Write your articles in draft mode, and when you are ready to go live, simply flip the switch to publish them.

New articles and documents are automatically added to the full text search engine, and submitted to Google for indexing.

  • Generates exceptionally clean code
  • Includes basic formatting
  • Image upload and linking functions included

Created with SEO in mind

We know many of your customers will start their search for help on Google. If your support information does not appear in the results, you are fielding customer inquiries you don't need to.

Your Helpy knowledgebase comes ready for Google, with an emphasis on clean, fast loading code and mobile friendly design.

  • Set keywords, meta-description and alternate title tag
  • Clean code
  • Title used in URL
  • Link text includes keywords
