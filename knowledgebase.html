A simple tool to build, mantain and improve your knowledgbase

A modern wysiwyg editor (Quill.js) is your gateway to creating amazing articles to delight your users. Write your articles in draft mode, and when you are ready to go live, simply flip the switch to publish them.

New articles and documents are automatically added to the full text search engine, and submitted to Google for indexing.

Don't worry about making a mistake, every version of each article is archived and can be recovered with a mouse click.

Full text search that will help your customers get answers faster!

Customers that search have the best chance of answering their own question, and provide you with valuable feedback on what their "real" questions are.

Helpy full text search is powered by Postgres, although you are welcome to use your own backend.

Created with SEO in mind

We know many of your customers will start their search for help on Google. If your support information does not appear in the results, you are fielding customer inquiries you don't need to.

Your Helpy knowledgebase comes ready for Google, with an emphasis on clean, fast loading code and mobile friendly design. Supply your own keywords, title tags and meta descriptions, and let our internal linking structure help your pages rise to the top of the search results.

Manage and see reports on everything via Google Analytics, even in "Real time."

Rather than rely on an internal reporting system, Helpy is full integrated with Google Analytics. Track and view reports on how many tickets are coming in, what the productivity of your agents is, and more. Download or contribute custom dashboards via the GA exchange on our support site.
