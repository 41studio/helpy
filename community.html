Empower your customers to assist each other

Helpy community discussions work just like support tickets, but any customer can answer, and interactions take place through the web.

An always expanding database of real customer solutions

You set up the categories and your users provide the questions and answers! Your community forum is a place where customers can interact with each other, to share solutions, tips and advice.

Every conversation is searchable and available to customers with a similar problem.

[image]
[image]

Offload support responsibility to your best customer advocates

Save money on support costs by sharing the burden with your strongest advocates- current customers!

If there is a question which is not being answered promptly, support agents can jump in and provide an answer. In fact, they see public discussions in the same UI as private tickets.

Keep control and provide assistance when needed

Don't worry about things getting out of control, you still have complete control over the community discussions. Delete posts or deactivate discussions which are off topic, promotional or negative.

You or your customers can also convert a discussion to a private ticket with a single click.
