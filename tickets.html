All your customer communications in one place, accessible from anywhere

Support ticketing keeps your team synced and delivering a wonderful customer experience

Manage the complete support lifecycle, from request to resolution

Helpy support discussions can be assigned to members of your team, and tracked by status throughout their life.

Helpy "gets" email, literally.

Helpy connects to your incoming support email addresses, and gives you and your team an organized tool for managing all of your customer communications in one place.

Google Analytics is "Baked In"

Rather than rely on an internal reporting system, Helpy is full integrated with Google Analytics. Track and view reports on how many tickets are coming in, as well as the productivity of your agents.
