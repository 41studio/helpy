Open source help desk software that will delight your customers, and your wallet

Introducing Helpy.
A modern, open-source Helpdesk alternative.

Ticketing

Helpy features a complete ticketing system, fully integrated with customer support email.

Knowledgebase

Easily create a searchable knowledgebase to cut down on support tickets.

Community

Your customers can post their questions into the community as well, getting answers and tips from other users!

More Reasons to Put Helpy to Work for You

Google Analytics Baked In

Other tools give you a reporting system. Helpy is fully integrated with GA, the tool you would rather get your data from, right?

Make it Your Own

Helpy is open source software, ready for you to just use, or customize and extend as far as you want to take it. Run it, hack it, use it!

Pay Nothing

No monthly fees, no per agent seat costs, and not even a hosting bill if you stay under 1000 tickets per month (our best guess, YMMV)

Integrated Full Text Search

Out of the box, Helpy is preconfigured to use Postgres full text search, to help your users find solutions to their problems before contacting you.

Works on any Device

You and your agents aim to provide support from anywhere. And Helpy will help you by working on any device- mobile, tablet or desktop.

Speaks your Language

Helpy comes with front-end translation packs for German, French, Spanish and Russian. It is easy to add your own internationalization.

How do I get it?

Helpy is open source software, and is made available on Github. Feel free to grab a copy, install and play with it.
